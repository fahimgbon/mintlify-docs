### Why Microsoft?
- Building on Microsoft Accessibility Conformance Reports for internal testing
- Resources to affordably provide AI suggestions on site improvements
- Integrated into the Chromium architecture, ensuring:
    - Access to large customer base 
    - Improved UX through familiar ecosystem
    - Intuitive and minimalist UI
- _Later: Open source_ 



### Hypothesis
Implementing accessibility guidelines during the software development life cycle will result in a more positive user experience for individuals with accessibility needs, as measured by increased user satisfaction, reduced frustration, and improved task completion rates.

### Success Metrics
- Reduced # of home pages with detectable WCAG failures
- Increasing score rating YoY
- Increased in usage metrics (clicks & frequency)

### Risks & Mitigations
- Privacy Concerns -> User awareness on data being collected
- Quantitative Accuracy -> Transparency on scoring methodology and data to provide justification
- Adoption -> Initial targeting towards metaverse products looking to create immersive experiences
