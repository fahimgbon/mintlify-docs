<Steps>
  <Step title="Develop a product requirement document">
  This document outlines the project's goals, objectives, budget, timeline, stakeholders, and key milestones.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Break down the project into work packages:">
   Dividing the larger project into smaller, manageable tasks with defined deliverables.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Estimate time and resources required:">
  Forecasting the effort and resources needed for each work package.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Develop a project schedule">
  Creating a roadmap with timelines, dependencies between tasks, and milestones.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Communication Plan">
  Defining how information will be shared among stakeholders and team members: Async and sychronous communication
  </Step>
</Steps>
