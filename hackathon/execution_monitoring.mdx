<Steps>
  <Step title="Assign Tasks and Responsibilities">
  Distributing user stories to team members based on their expertise and availability.
  
  Tool: Scrum Board, Microsoft Loop:
  - To do
  - In Progress
  - Done
  </Step>
  <Step title="Launch Project Tasks">
  Putting the plan into action, starting work on assigned tasks.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Monitor Progress and Performance">
  Tool: Daily Scrum Board, Microsoft Loop:

  1. What did you do yesterday?
  2. What will you do today?
  3. Are there any blockers?
  </Step>
</Steps>
